Parent and school interaction at various events and meet organized by the school is vital. This interaction involves the overall development of various skills in the child including emotional, physical, social, Intellectual and linguistic. Joint efforts of the school and the parents result in all-round development of the child. SKIRS firmly believes in building up healthy communication and close rapport between the parents and teachers. We believe in working as a team and make sure that there is no gap in communication. We look at the role of parents as equal partners in the child’s growth.

Parents are kept informed of all school events through a newsletter. They follow their child’s progress through regular and detailed school reports. The school also welcomes parents to visit the school to discuss matters of concern with House Tutor / house-parents. This way we ensure that everyone contributes to the outstanding development of students.

Parent teacher meetings are an important forum to forge the synergy between the school and the students. Interaction between parents and teachers is one done on a regular basis. This is the life line between staff and parents with regard to the day-to-day progress of every student.
