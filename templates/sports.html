The sports academy at SKIRS is a natural extension of the all-round development motto promoted by the school. Physical Education is given due importance to enhance and nourish the multi benefits of sports like a healthy body, sound mind, emotional stability, mental agility, social and moral values like integrity, honesty, gamesmanship, team spirit and winning attitude.

SKIRS sports academy also aims to build a pool of sporting talent and provides for specialized coaching by qualified coaches for many indoor and outdoor games and sports. It is well-equipped with outdoor fields and indoor sports complex and a modern multi gymnasium for physical fitness and mental conditioning.

SKIRS SPORTS FEATURES

SKIRS Sports Academy trains individuals with an opportunity to compete at state, zonal and national events in sports such as


  • Cricket
  • Football
  • Lawn Tennis
  • Basket Ball
  • Volley Ball
  • Skating
  • Swimming
  • Martial Arts
  • Yoga & Meditation
  • Table Tennis/ Chess/ Carrom
  • Hockey
  • Billiards

SKIRS SPORTS OBJECTIVES

The SKIRS Sports academy has clear defined objectives to function both as a support entity to engage students in physical education and also to create a pool of sporting talent.

  • To create awareness of the importance of Sports.
  • To provide all- round development of the child.
  • To enhance character building and ethics through sports.
  • To inculcate leadership qualities and promote team-spirit.
  • To provide world-class infrastructure and intensive training facilities.
  • To provide sports event management training program.
